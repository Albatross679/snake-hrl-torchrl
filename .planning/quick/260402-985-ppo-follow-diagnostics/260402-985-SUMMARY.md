## Key Findings from Probes

### EXP1: Reward Landscape
- Mean reward: 0.049 (exp(-5d) at mean distance 0.78m)
- 70% of steps have reward > 0.01 -- not truly sparse but weak
- exp(-2d) would give 5x higher mean reward (0.245)
- exp(-1d) would give 10x higher (0.476)

### EXP2: Action Impact
- Zero actions: exactly 0 tip displacement (mock physics is deterministic)
- Random actions: 0.052m mean tip displacement per step
- State change ratio: 2.12 vs 0.005 -- actions have clear effect
- **H3 (mock physics broken) is REFUTED**

### EXP3: Reachability
- 90% of targets reachable within 0.3m with random actions
- Lower hemisphere easier (50% within 0.1m) vs upper (14%)
- **H4 (unreachable targets) is partially supported for upper hemisphere only**
