Pipe Contact Physics Visualization

A snake rod (blue) inside a pipe made of ring-shaped wall rods (gray). Contact forces (red arrows) push the snake away from walls.

Staggered Rings

Adjacent rings are rotated by half the angular step. Toggle stagger to see how gaps align without it vs. get covered with it.

Contact Model (IMC Penalty)

When distance Δ between a snake edge and wall edge falls below threshold δ, an exponential penalty force activates:
E = kc · h · (2v·ln(v+1)) / (K1(v+1))
where v = e-K1Δ. Smooth and C².
